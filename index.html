About The Fire Man

Hi I’m Andrew, owner of The Fire Man. We provide a comprehensive 1st class service in a transparent and friendly way. We run a small, independent fire safety business, providing domestic and commercial fire protection services across Cambridgeshire and the surrounding areas.

As times are hard and we are all feeling the pinch, you don’t need a life safety system to cost the earth! We are an independent low cost alternative to fire safety without sacrificing on quality.

We don’t have massive overheads to deal with as we keep the company small, friendly and cost efficient. No need for large Companies, office staff, fleets of vans, shareholders. This is why our prices are low and with over 25 years experience we’re here to help in any way we can.

We focus on providing friendly, high standard fire protection servicing, equipment and guidance to business owners.

Thank you for considering The Fire Man, please do not hesitate to reach out if you have any questions. We look forward to hearing from you.
